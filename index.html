ABOUT


Vietnamese food is considered one of the healthiest cuisines worldwide due to its use of fresh herbs, vegetables and meats. Authentic Vietnamese cooking relies on an abundance of fresh vegetables and ingredients with minimal use of dairy and oil. The Banh Mi Shop is committed to serve traditional healthy Vietnamese food at a reasonable price. Our dishes are packed with flavor by using fresh clean vegetables, herbs, spices and ingredients loaded with nutrients, vitamins and minerals. We take advantage of the many fresh flavors of Vietnamese cuisine and prepare our food with the most healthy cooking methods. All of our dishes are hand crafted the way the Le family has been cooking at home for generations. We want to consistently provide our customers with delicious food and impeccable service. Our mission at Monarch 9 is to provide a healthy Vietnamese cuisine in a unique environment with friendly yet un-intrusive service.
